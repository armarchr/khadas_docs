title: Khadas Settings Usage Guide
---

Some setting functions Khadas added.

## Khadas Settings

`Settings` --> `Khadas settings`
<img src="/android/images/vim4/KhadasSettings1.png" width="75%" height="75%">

## Settings Menu Lists

### HDMI IN
Goto HDMI input screen.

### Ethernet
The relevant settings of ethernet.

### Display & Sound 
Backlight adjustment and more settings such as screen direction and density.
<img src="/android/images/vim4/KhadasSettings2.png" width="75%" height="75%">

### PowerKey definition
The power button can be defined as sleep, shutdown and restart.
<img src="/android/images/vim4/KhadasSettings3.png" width="75%" height="75%">

### FunctionKey definition
The default function key is HOME, it can be filled in according to the corresponding table of Android standard key code value.
<img src="/android/images/vim4/KhadasSettings4.png" width="75%" height="75%">

### LEDs control
Control white led and red led in power on state.
<img src="/android/images/vim4/KhadasSettings5.png" width="75%" height="75%">

### Cooling fan
The user-defined control of the fan. By default, it is automatically adjusted according to the temperature.
<img src="/android/images/vim4/KhadasSettings6.png" width="75%" height="75%">

### WOL
Wake on Ethernet (WOL) allows you to wake up the device through LAN or WAN with your mobile phone or computer. Please refer to [WOL](Wol.html).

### Status bar control
Hide or display of the top and bottom status bar.
<img src="/android/images/vim4/KhadasSettings7.png" width="75%" height="75%">

### Logo LED control
The control of LED lights on the expansion board.
<img src="/android/images/vim4/KhadasSettings8.png" width="75%" height="75%">

### Timer
Set timing shutdown and timing startup.
<img src="/android/images/vim4/KhadasSettings9.png" width="75%" height="75%">

### Updater
The VIM4 online upgrade function.
